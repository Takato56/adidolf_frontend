## AI Usage Policy
Use of AI is allowed in this project under the following policies:
### All usage of AI must be recorded in ai-logs/ in the md file corresponding to the current week. This includes:
- Reason/Purpose (What you intended to do)
- Tool(s) used (Figma, Codex,...)
- Prompt used (if any)
- What the AI outputted (summary)
- Output evaluation (Is the output satisfactory? Does it adhere to the prompt?)
- Reflection (From this use, what did you learn? Such as how to better prompt, how to follow up, how to critique the output or what insights you gained in software development)
### Understanding of the Output
Understand what the AI is doing and know whether what it's doing is good or bad in the context of the entire project
