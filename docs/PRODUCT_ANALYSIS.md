## Product Analysis
### Target Audience
People seeking haute couture at an affordable price.

### The Problem
Haute couture brands like Chanel, Louis Vuitton and Tiffany offer luxurious symbols of status at an equally unaffordable price. People who seek to possess such items but don't have the money to afford them often resort to buying counterfeits instead. However, with the recent crackdowns in said counterfeits, these people are left high and dry, looking for a replacement. That's where we come in, we offer meticulously designed fashion at an affordable price to fill in this gap.

### Minimum Viable Product
An online fashion store/website should have these following basic functions: display information of available products, display contact information, allow for ordering products and track orders.
